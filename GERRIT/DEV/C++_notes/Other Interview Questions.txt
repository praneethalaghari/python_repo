What is a cookie..?

 - A cookie is a file which stores few info about the user preferences related to website.
    i.e Language,shopping preferences,visited websites
 - One websites cookies can conains bits of cookies of other website too.. 
 - Thats the reason why we get amazon,flipkart etc ads on other sites we access.
